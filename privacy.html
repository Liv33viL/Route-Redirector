Privacy Policy – Route Redirector

Route Redirector is a Chrome extension that lets you define simple HTTP/HTTPS redirect rules (including optional ports). All redirects are created and enforced locally in your browser. The extension does not collect, transmit, or share personal information.

Data Storage

The extension stores only the redirect rules you configure yourself (rule name, source host/port, destination scheme/host/port, enabled state) using Chrome’s storage.local API. This data never leaves your device, is not synced to external servers, and is not shared with third parties.

Permissions & Purpose

  • storage – needed solely to remember your redirect rules across browser sessions.
  • declarativeNetRequest – required to apply your rules to matching requests via Chrome’s built-in network rules engine.
  • host permissions (<all_urls>) – ensures the redirect rules can match any site you specify.

These permissions are used only to fulfill the extension’s single purpose: applying the redirects you configure.

Tracking & Analytics

The extension does not include telemetry, analytics, advertising, or remote code.
